To play the dice game, you will need nothing thats the beauty of the game. The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 total points.

Here are the basic rules:
  1. player 1 starts the game and gets a chance to roll the dice.
  2. If the player rolls a number between 2 and 6, they earn that many points for their current account.
  3. The player can choose to roll the dice again or end their turn by pressing the "hold" button.
  4. If the player presses the "hold" button, their current points are added to their total points and the next player takes turns following the same rules.
  5. If the player rolls a 1, their turn ends and the current point becomes 0, and no point is added to the total points.
  6. The game continues until a player reaches 100 total points.
